Pan-Seared Steak with Garlic Butter

This Pan-Seared Steak has a garlic butter that makes it taste like a steakhouse quality meal. You'll be impressed at how easy it is to make the perfect steak that's seared on the outside, and perfectly tender inside. Servings - 4 Prep Time: 5minutes mins Cook Time: 15minutes mins Total Time: 20minutes mins Ingredients: 2 lbs New York Strip Steaks (2 steaks), or Ribeye or Top Sirloin Steaks (1 lb each steak, 1.25 inches thick) 1/2 Tbsp vegetable oil, or any high heat cooking oil like canola or extra light olive oil 1 1/2 tsp sea salt 1 tsp black pepper, freshly ground 2 Tbsp unsalted butter 2 cloves garlic, peeled and quartered 1 sprig fresh rosemary
